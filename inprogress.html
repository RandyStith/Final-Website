Subnautica is a well known and very well recieved singleplayer survival videogame. It is based on a planet in a different galaxy than earth, and follows a crash survivor on planet 4546B.
In 2018 Subnautica won the "Breakthrough" and "PC Game Of The Year" awards in the Golden Joystick Awards, and won the "Fan Favorite Indie Game" award for the Gamer's Choice Awards. In 2019 Subnautia was nominated for similar awards.
There is no doubt that Subnautica is one of a kind, it was the first fully underwater survival game with a story at the time. It took them a while, but the people at Unknown Worlds Entertainment struck gold with this iconic game.
